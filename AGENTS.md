## Imported Claude Cowork project instructions

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
TX Localist is a paid directory platform where small, local businesses purchase month-to-month listings to increase visibility within their city.
Consumers (“Localists”) use the website and mobile app to:
•	Search for local businesses by city and keyword
•	View listings
•	Save favorite businesses
•	Browse local job postings
The platform must be scalable, modular, and designed for phased expansion.
